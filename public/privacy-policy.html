Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 19, 2024
Your Privacy Matters: At InnerBalance, we understand that financial and emotional data is deeply personal. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, protect, and respect your information while providing our AI-powered financial therapy and coaching services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Financial Information

  • Expense data you categorize as "Living" vs "Lifestyle" expenses through our Expense Highlighter tool
  • Financial timeline information including past influences, present snapshot, and future goals via Financial GPS
  • Emergency fund and financial planning data
  • Self-reported financial behaviors and patterns

1.2 Emotional and Psychological Data

  • IFS dialogue responses identifying your financial "firefighter" parts
  • Self-compassion practice data and calm scores
  • Emotional responses and coping mechanisms related to financial stress
  • Trauma-informed coaching session data
  • Panic button usage and grounding exercise participation
  • Self-critical statement tracking over time

1.3 Technical Information

  • Device information and browser type
  • Usage patterns and interaction data with our AI tools
  • Session duration and feature usage analytics
  • Error logs and performance data

2. How We Use Your Information

2.1 Primary Purposes

  • Provide personalized AI-driven financial therapy and coaching
  • Generate insights about your financial "firefighter" parts using IFS methodology
  • Track your emotional and financial wellness progress
  • Deliver compassionate, trauma-informed financial guidance
  • Customize self-compassion prompts and mindfulness exercises

2.2 AI Processing

  • Our AI analyzes your input to provide personalized coaching responses
  • Pattern recognition to identify financial behaviors and emotional triggers
  • Generate supportive messaging aligned with your emotional state
  • Assess communication strategies based on your personality type

3. Data Security and Protection

Security First: Given the sensitive nature of financial and emotional data, we implement enterprise-grade security measures including end-to-end encryption, secure data storage, and regular security audits.

3.1 Technical Safeguards

  • End-to-end encryption for all data transmission
  • Encrypted storage of all personal information
  • Regular security vulnerability assessments
  • Multi-factor authentication for account access
  • Secure cloud infrastructure with industry-standard protections

3.2 Access Controls

  • Strict employee access controls on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular access reviews and audit trails
  • Confidentiality agreements for all staff members
  • Specialized training on handling sensitive psychological data

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 We DO NOT Share Your Data For:

  • Marketing or advertising purposes
  • Sale to third-party data brokers
  • Credit reporting or financial profiling
  • Social media integration or sharing

4.2 Limited Sharing Only When:

  • Required by law or legal process
  • You provide explicit written consent
  • To protect someone from imminent harm (following applicable mental health protocols)
  • To trusted service providers who assist in app functionality (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Your Rights and Control

5.1 Access and Control

  • View all data we have collected about you
  • Download your data in a portable format
  • Correct inaccurate information
  • Delete specific data entries or your entire account
  • Opt out of certain data processing activities

5.2 Panic Mode Protection

  • Instant data hiding capabilities through our "Red-Dot Panic" feature
  • Immediate access to grounding exercises without data retention
  • Option to pause all data collection during emotional distress

6. Special Considerations for Mental Health Data

Ethical Commitment: We recognize that our app processes sensitive mental health information. We adhere to ethical guidelines for digital mental health tools and maintain the highest standards of care.

6.1 Professional Standards

  • We follow trauma-informed care principles in all data handling
  • Our AI is designed to maintain a non-judgmental, compassionate approach
  • We do not replace professional mental health treatment
  • Clear boundaries between AI coaching and professional therapy

6.2 Crisis Support

  • We provide resources for professional mental health support
  • Crisis detection protocols that prioritize user safety
  • Clear pathways to human support when needed

7. Data Retention

7.1 Active Account

  • We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • You can delete specific entries at any time
  • Regular data purging of temporary session information

7.2 Account Deletion

  • Complete data deletion within 30 days of account termination
  • Backup data permanently removed within 90 days
  • Legal retention requirements may apply in limited circumstances

8. Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for users under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 18. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it immediately.

9. International Users

If you are accessing our service from outside the United States, please be aware that your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers.

10. Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy to reflect changes in our practices or for legal compliance. We will notify you of any material changes through the app and provide at least 30 days notice before implementation.

  • Email: privacy@innerbalance.app
  • Privacy Officer: For data protection inquiries
  • Response Time: We respond to privacy inquiries within 72 hours
Your Trust is Sacred: We understand that choosing to share your financial and emotional journey with us requires tremendous trust. We honor that trust by maintaining the highest standards of privacy protection and ethical data handling.
